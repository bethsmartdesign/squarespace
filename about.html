ABOUT

I have designed this demo website to showcase one of my concepts for the D&AD Squarespace compeition brief. The brief here was to create a portfolio template, which showcases the work of artists, designers and photographers.

My creative interests lie in graphic and web design. The goal I set myself for this academic year was to gain a broader coding knowledge to expand my web design and development skills. The web has always been an interest of mine and now being at university I feel this is an area I am able to learn about and advance in. I feel this Squarespace brief has allowed me to focus on new web design skills and begin to achieve my goal.
